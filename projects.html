Spotify Hot 100 Hits Analysis

Designed an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Spotify's Hot 100 songs from 1958–2024. Explored trends in musical characteristics such as BPM, energy, danceability, and acousticness while investigating shifts in artist diversity, genre popularity, chart longevity, and music consumption patterns over time. Created multiple visualizations including bar charts, line graphs, scatterplots, maps, and interactive filters to uncover long-term trends in the music industry.

Landscaping Services Marketplace Design

Designed an end-to-end marketplace platform in Figma that connects homeowners with local landscaping businesses. Inspired by platforms such as Airbnb and VRBO, the solution allows users to browse, compare, and connect with service providers based on location, services offered, ratings, and availability. Developed user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes focused on creating an intuitive booking experience while improving trust and accessibility for both customers and landscaping professionals.

Interactive LED Mason Jar

Developed an Arduino-powered LED mason jar that combines electronics, programming, and creative design. The project uses a push-button input to control multiple lighting modes, allowing users to cycle through different colors and effects. Designed as an interactive ambient light, the mason jar demonstrates circuit design, Arduino programming, and hands-on prototyping skills.
